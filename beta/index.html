Beta program foundation

This is the first operational intake surface for StreamSuites beta testers. It is intentionally early, honest, and structured around useful testers rather than broad promises.

Who we want early

People with creator-tool fluency, SaaS UX instincts, calm bug reporting habits, and a willingness to test rough edges carefully.

What this is not

This is not paid support, a guaranteed launch list, or a promise that every requested workflow is already live.

What helps most

Clear reproduction notes, environment detail, practical product feedback, and disciplined follow-through across a few test cycles.

Apply when you can contribute signal

If you understand creator tooling, operational workflows, QA discipline, or technical product testing, the beta application route is ready.
