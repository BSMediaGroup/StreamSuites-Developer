`StreamSuites-Developer v0.5.0-alpha` brings the Developer Console to solid first-class surface status with full shell parity, structured reporting, and proper auth surface identification.

- Expanded surface catalog covering all major StreamSuites products and runtime targets.

- Turnstile + access-code gate parity with public surfaces.
- Access-class and developer_console_access enforcement.
